About Clickstat

Clickstat is a URL shortener with IP and GPS logging. Unlike other IP loggers that provide inaccurate location data, Clickstat utilizes GPS for pinpoint accuracy. Shorten your links with "GPS Tracking" enabled and get GPS location of those who click it. it's a powerful tool that empowers you to shorten links, while also gaining insights into the individuals who interact with them. With Clickstat, you can access valuable information such as IP addresses, user agents, and, if enabled, precise GPS locations of your link's clickers.
