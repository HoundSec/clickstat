About Clickstat

Clickstat is more than just a URL shortener; it's a powerful tool that empowers you to shorten links, while also gaining insights into the individuals who interact with them. With Clickstat, you can access valuable information such as IP addresses, user agents, and, if enabled, precise GPS locations of your link's clickers. Unlike other IP loggers that provide inaccurate location data, Clickstat utilizes GPS for pinpoint accuracy. Experience the best and most feature-packed URL shortener on the internet.
