Welcome to Clickstat - An URL Shortener with IP and GPS logging

Clickstat is more than just a URL shortener; it's a powerful tool that lets you shorten links, while also gaining insights into the individuals who interact with them. With Clickstat, you can access valuable information such as IP addresses, user agents, and, if enabled, precise GPS locations of your link's clickers. Unlike other IP loggers that provide inaccurate location data, Clickstat utilizes GPS for pinpoint accuracy.

How to Use Clickstat:

Step 1: Shorten Your Link
1. Paste your long URL into the provided form.
2. Check the "Enable GPS Tracking" option if you want to log GPS data.
3. Click the "Shorten" button.
You'll receive a shortened URL and a unique identifier. The shortened URL is what you share, and the identifier is your key to accessing data about those who click on your link.

Keep in mind that if you enable GPS Tracking, the individual who clicks the link will be asked to grant GPS permission for privacy and security reasons. If they allow it, they will gain access to the link, and we will log their location. If they don't grant permission, they won't be able to access the link, and we won't log their information. If you don't want any permission pop-ups don't use GPS logging. You will still be able view information like IP addresses and user agents!

Step 2: View User/Click Data
To access detailed statistics about the individuals who clicked your link:
1. Navigate to the stats page on Clickstat.
2. Enter the identifier associated with your link.
3. Click the "View" button to access the stats.

Why Choose Clickstat?

1. Speed and Reliability: Clickstat is designed for efficiency. Your links are shortened quickly, ensuring a seamless experience for both you and your audience.
2. Data Privacy: We take your privacy seriously. Unlike other services, we do not sell your data to advertising providers. Your information is safe and secure with Clickstat.
